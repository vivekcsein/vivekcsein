# AR Industry Overview

Augmented Reality (AR) Filters have evolved from simple face masks into interactive experiences used for:

- Brand Marketing
- Product Launches
- Entertainment
- Gaming
- Education
- Virtual Shopping
- Social Media Campaigns
- Playable Advertisements
- Events
- Sports Engagement
- Music Promotion

Major brands now use AR as an engagement tool instead of traditional advertisements.

Typical commercial project pricing:

| Project Complexity  |    Estimated Cost |
| ------------------- | ----------------: |
| Simple Face Filter  |       $300 – $800 |
| Interactive Filter  |     $800 – $2,000 |
| Advanced Game Lens  |   $2,000 – $8,000 |
| Enterprise Campaign | $8,000 – $20,000+ |

Leading studios such as **AliveNow**, **Frenzz Studio**, and global creative agencies regularly deliver projects in the **$2,000–$20,000+** range depending on complexity.

# Main Categories of AR Filters

# 1. World AR Filters

World AR places digital content into the real environment using surface detection, depth, SLAM, GPS, or landmark tracking.

## Features

- Plane Detection
- Surface Detection
- Physics
- Occlusion
- Real Scale Objects
- Lighting Estimation
- Persistent Anchors

## Examples

### Object Placement

- Place furniture
- Place vehicles
- Place statues
- Place products

Example:

- IKEA furniture preview

---

### Landmark Experiences

Large experiences attached to famous buildings.

Examples

- Eiffel Tower animation
- Taj Mahal storytelling
- Stadium AR
- Tourism campaigns

---

### Portal Experiences

Walk through a magical doorway into another world.

Examples

- Fantasy portal
- Museum portal
- Historical recreation
- Movie promotion

---

### Physics-Based World Filters

Objects react to gravity and collisions.

Examples

- Throw basketball
- Stack cubes
- Falling confetti
- Domino simulation

---

### Product Showcase

3D product demonstrations.

Examples

- Shoe showcase
- Phone showcase
- Cosmetics
- Watch preview

---

### Unboxing Experience

Animated product packaging.

Examples

- Phone launch
- Gaming hardware
- Food packaging
- Gift boxes

---

### Navigation Experiences

AR arrows guiding users.

Examples

- Mall navigation
- Museum guide
- Event navigation
